Our Expertise

ASIC Design

We provide ASIC engineers skilled in RTL design, microarchitecture, SoC integration, and low-power implementation. Our teams support advanced nodes (7nm and below) and specialize in integrating ML accelerators, high-speed interfaces, and custom compute blocks into production silicon.

FPGA & Prototyping

Our FPGA engineers develop and validate complex designs across major platforms (Xilinx, Intel, Lattice). Capabilities include hardware prototyping of AI inference pipelines, HLS-based acceleration, board bring-up, and pre-silicon validation using emulation and hybrid simulation flows.

Functional Verification

We offer comprehensive verification expertise: UVM, SystemVerilog, Portable Stimulus (PSS), formal verification, and coverage-driven methodology. Our teams validate AI/ML subsystems, custom memory hierarchies, interconnects, and complex SoCs from block to system level.

Embedded Software & Edge AI

We support embedded development across C/C++, Linux, RTOS, and bare-metal environments. Our engineers have deep expertise in compiler toolchains, NPU firmware, DMA orchestration, and ML inference frameworks (TFLite, TVM, ONNX), enabling real-time edge AI on custom silicon platforms.

Physical Design

Our physical design engineers specialize in place & route, timing closure, DFT, and signoff for advanced process nodes. We ensure manufacturability, power efficiency, and robust silicon performance for complex SoCs and custom ASICs.

PCB

We offer PCB design and layout services for high-speed digital, mixed-signal, and RF boards. Our team handles schematic capture, signal integrity, power delivery, and manufacturing support for prototypes and production runs.

Analog IC Layout

Our analog layout engineers deliver precision layout for analog, mixed-signal, and RF ICs. Expertise includes low-noise amplifiers, data converters, power management, and custom analog blocks for demanding applications.

FPGA DevOps

We implement FPGA DevOps pipelines for automated build, test, and deployment of FPGA designs. Our solutions integrate CI/CD, version control, simulation, synthesis, and hardware validation to accelerate development cycles and ensure reproducibility.

Based in Ruse, Bulgaria

Based in Ruse, Bulgaria—at the crossroads of Central and Eastern Europe—we harness the region's 70-year legacy in computing and semiconductors, along with a highly skilled engineering workforce from both Bulgaria and neighboring Romania.

Our strategic location enables smooth, nearshore collaboration with European Blue Chip companies and agile startups alike, while maintaining cost-efficiency and operational flexibility.

We are committed to delivering world-class semiconductor solutions with the precision, reliability, and quality expected from a European engineering partner.
