Our Expertise

ASIC Design

We provide ASIC engineers skilled in RTL design, microarchitecture, SoC integration, and low-power implementation. Our teams support advanced nodes (7nm and below) and specialize in integrating ML accelerators, high-speed interfaces, and custom compute blocks into production silicon.

FPGA & Prototyping

Our FPGA engineers develop and validate complex designs across major platforms (Xilinx, Intel, Lattice). Capabilities include hardware prototyping of AI inference pipelines, HLS-based acceleration, board bring-up, and pre-silicon validation using emulation and hybrid simulation flows.

Functional Verification

We offer comprehensive verification expertise: UVM, SystemVerilog, Portable Stimulus (PSS), formal verification, and coverage-driven methodology. Our teams validate AI/ML subsystems, custom memory hierarchies, interconnects, and complex SoCs from block to system level.

Embedded Software & Edge AI

We support embedded development across C/C++, Linux, RTOS, and bare-metal environments. Our engineers have deep expertise in compiler toolchains, NPU firmware, DMA orchestration, and ML inference frameworks (TFLite, TVM, ONNX), enabling real-time edge AI on custom silicon platforms.
